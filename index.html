About Us

We specialize in strong, galvanized, stylish readymade designs and custom gates fitted to your design, as well as heavy-duty trailers built to last. Clients can bring their own design and our expert team brings them to life. Prices range from R1,800 to R18,000 depending on design. With more than 25 years of experience, we combine precision craftsmanship and quality materials to deliver reliable solutions or every project.
